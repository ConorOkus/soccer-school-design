About Us

Front Foot is a soccer school based in South California with the aim to promote a progressive, attractive style of play to its players. Front Foot is all about attacking situations with a readiness and confidence destined for success. Our team is run by a current MLS academy head coach, giving an extremely high level of coaching and experience. Front Foot aims to develop a growth mindset that can be used to apply good coaching techniques wherever their soccer career may take them.
